Dream Water

February 19, 2026 • 1 min read
Scholar Reviewed Content

TL;DR

Water is the essence of life and represents knowledge, faith, and purity. Drinking clear, cool water signifies gaining wisdom and spiritual growth. However, drowning in water can represent being overwhelmed by worldly affairs or sins. Turbulent or muddy water is a warning of trials, sickness, or injustice. If you see water flooding your house, it represents family disputes. Moving from stagnant water to running water symbolizes a transition from depression to activity and hope.
