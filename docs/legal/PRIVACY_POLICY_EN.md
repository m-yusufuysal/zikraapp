# Islamvy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Islamvy App ("the App") collects, processes, stores, and protects your data in accordance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK Law No. 6698) and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Data Controller

**Organization**: Islamvy App (Islamvy Team)  
**Contact Email**: privacy@islamvyapp.com  
**Location**: UAE (United Arab Emirates)

---

## 2. Personal Data We Process

To provide the best service and improve your experience, the App collects the following data:

### 2.1 Basic Account & Identity Information
- **Email Address**: For registration, login, and account security.
- **Full Name**: For in-app personalization and community interaction.
- **Profile Picture (Media)**: Avatars uploaded by the user (stored on the Supabase Storage platform).

### 2.2 Special Category (Sensitive) Data
Under KVKK and GDPR, data related to religion and beliefs is considered "Sensitive." This data is processed only with your explicit consent and for the purpose of providing the service (dhikr plans, worship tracking, etc.):
- **Location Data (Temporary)**: For calculating prayer times and Qibla direction (Not stored, processed in real-time).
- **Spiritual Intentions & Goals**: To generate AI-powered dhikr plans.
- **Prayer & Dhikr Participation**: Your spiritual interactions within the community.

### 2.3 Payment & Subscription Data
- **Subscription Status**: Premium membership status (Starter, Pro, Unlimited) and validity period.
- **Referral Codes**: Codes used under the influencer program and related click data.

### 2.4 Usage & Technical Data
- **Device Tokens**: For sending Push Notifications (Expo/Firebase).
- **IP Address & Device Information**: For security, debugging, and fraud prevention.
- **AI Moderation Data**: Data from AI-based checks carried out to ensure the safety of text and images sent to the community.

---

## 3. Data Processing Purposes and Legal Bases

Your data is processed based on the following:
1. **Performance of Contract**: Account creation, subscription management, and core features.
2. **Explicit Consent**: AI dream interpretation, personalized dhikr plans, and community shares.
3. **Legitimate Interest**: App security (moderation), analytics, and service quality improvement.
4. **Legal Obligation**: Meeting requests from judicial authorities and maintaining financial records.

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Transfer

Your data is shared only with our trusted service providers to the extent necessary for service delivery:

- **Supabase Inc.** (USA): Database management, authentication, media storage, and real-time notifications.
- **OpenAI Inc.** (USA): AI features and content safety (Moderation).
- **Google AdMob** (USA): Advertising services and performance analytics.
- **Amazon Affiliate**: Store redirects and click tracking (Amazon policies of the respective country apply).

*Note: All US-based providers are certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.*

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account.
- **Media Files**: Your profile photo is permanently deleted when you delete your account or remove the photo yourself.
- **AI Inputs**: Inputs entered for dreams or dhikr are anonymized or deleted within a maximum of 30 days after the process is complete.

**Deleting Your Data**: You can permanently delete all your data and stored media with one click by following the path **Settings > Delete My Account** within the app.

---

## 6. User Rights

Under KVKK Article 11 and GDPR, you have the following rights:
- To learn whether your data is being processed.
- To access your data and request a copy (Data Portability).
- To request correction of inaccurate data.
- To request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").

To exercise these rights, please email **privacy@islamvyapp.com**.

---

## 7. Data Security

- ✅ All data traffic is encrypted with **HTTPS/TLS**.
- ✅ Sensitive data is protected with **AES-256** in the database.
- ✅ User data is isolated only to the relevant user via **Row Level Security (RLS)**.
